Luxury Apartment Rudnik

AVAILABLE FOR SALE

For sale is a practically new, extremely bright, comfortable, modern and luxuriously furnished 3 bedroom apartment (total area 99,9 m2), in a newer building, built in 2021 in Ljubljana, Rudnik. The apartment is made of quality materials and fully equipped with high-end furnishings. The apartment is located on the 2nd floor (lift), has a beautiful view of the Polhograjske dolomites and comprises a large living room with kitchen and dining area, exit to the balcony (8.9 m2), master bedroom, additional room or bedroom, utility, main bathroom (2 sinks, shower and bath) with electric toilet / bidet and a living room toilet. There is also a storage room (2,6 m2) and a covered parking space. Heating by heat pump, cooling by air conditioning. Very good insulation, triple-glazed windows, underfloor heating, low living and maintenance costs. ZK condition regulated. The apartment has a shared use of a large fenced atrium in which there is a park and a children's playground. Equipment: 2 x LCD TV / induction / fridge with freezer / electric hood / dishwasher / dryer / washing machine / air conditioning / ventilation with heat recovery / electric blinds / air conditioning / LED lighting in all rooms
